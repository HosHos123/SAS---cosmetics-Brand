##  Project Overview
[SAS Natura is a marketing project focused on building a strong and competitive cosmetics brand in the natural beauty industry. The project aims to analyze the market, identify target customers, and create a complete digital marketing strategy. It addresses the challenge of standing out in a highly competitive cosmetics market by using data-driven insights, creative content, and paid advertising strategies. The final presentation will include a full marketing plan, competitor analysis, and campaign execution strategy..]


## Project Objectives
1.	Build a clear brand identity for SAS Natura 
2.	Analyze the cosmetics market and identify competitors 
3.	Define and understand the target audience 
4.	Create a complete digital marketing strategy 
5.	Develop effective paid advertising campaigns 
6.	Produce engaging and relevant content strategy 

##  Project Scope
1.	Market research and competitor analysis 
2.	Target audience segmentation 
3.	Brand positioning and messaging 
4.	Content creation strategy 
5.	Paid advertising plan (Meta & Google Ads) 
6.	Performance measurement and optimization 

>  **Final Deliverable:** 
A complete digital marketing strategy including market analysis, content plan, and paid advertising campaign ready for execution.
